Wearable Haptic Navigation Device

For the visually impaired

Worked with a team of two others to make a haptic navigation device for the visually impaired. Combines an Arduino, two motors, and an ultrasonic sensor onto a hat to provide intuitive feedback of how far an object is from the user. The closer an object is, the more frequent and intense the motor pulses.
